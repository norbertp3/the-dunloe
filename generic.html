About The Dunloe

The Dunloe Hotel & Gardens is a beautiful 5* hotel, overlooking the famous Gap of Dunloe, set on its own 64 acre estate leading to the ruins of the 12th century castle and River Laune. The dog-friendly The Dunloe Hotel & Gardens is one of the finest luxury hotels Ireland has, boasting magnificent 5 star hotel accommodation in Kerry, only 5 miles from Killarney town. The estate manicured pathways lead to the ruins of the old castle which lies hidden within the grounds.
