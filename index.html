A 5-star Irish Hotel that Nothing Comes Close To ‒ The Dunloe Hotel & Gardens

Discover The Dunloe Hotel & Gardens, Ireland’s unique five star luxury hotel that captivates with tranquil sophistication and natural splendour at the breathtaking Gap of Dunloe. The 64-acre estate transports you away from the everyday with its sprawling manicured parklands showcasing an extraordinary arboretum that leads on to the hidden ruins of the 13th century Dunloe Castle. Ancient and modern worlds meet amid the spectacular rugged beauty of the River Laune and County Kerry scenery, with a stay at The Dunloe like nowhere else on earth.

Your Special Day

Only at The Dunloe, one of the best five star hotels in Ireland, can you experience Irish history, adventure, nature’s playground, world-class dining, stylish architecture, couples’ activities, and the benefits of a family friendly hotel. Our discerning guests are invited to enjoy complimentary onsite leisure activities including horse riding, tennis, fishing, swimming, sauna, steam room, and fitness studio. This famed region will meet every need with golf, tours and walks able to be arranged locally, while family friendly options such as The D Club and movie night programme run during the summer months.
